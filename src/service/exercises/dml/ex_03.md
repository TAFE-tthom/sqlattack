## Updating Prices

It looks like prices are going up! For the business to make more of a profit, the prices of the products below will be increasing. You will need to write multiple update statements

Remember the columns;

* `Product`
  * `product_id`, `INT`
  * `name`, `VARCHAR(40)`
  * `price`, `DECIMAL`

Here is the list of products to enter in, you have been given IDs this time.

| ID | Name                        | Old  | New |
| -- | ----------------------------|------|-----| 
| 3  | Cola - With Sugar           | 5.5  | 6.0 |
| 5  | Jelly Beans (Not Edible)    | 2.25 | 2.5 |
| 10 | Blank CD                    | 12.55| 13.5|
| 12 | Blank DVD                   | 7.5  | 8.25|


You can have multiple update statements, make sure you update each item using their id.
