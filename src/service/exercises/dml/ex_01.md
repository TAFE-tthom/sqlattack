## Insert a product

As with your duties here, you need to start adding in product entries into the database. You have been given a table with the following columns.

* `Product`
  * `product_id`, `INT`
  * `name`, `VARCHAR(40)`
  * `price`, `DECIMAL`

You will need to insert the following product:

* Name: `Blank Video Tape`
* Price: `4.50`

The `product_id` can be selected by you, however you will need to include it in your answer.
