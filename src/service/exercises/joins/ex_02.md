## Customers and Rentals


You have been tasked with finding out what rentals each customer has committed.

As a hint, you should use at least 1 `LEFT JOIN` in this instance as you want to outline all customers regardless if they have made a rental or not.

Your query should output the following columns:

* `first_name`
* `last_name`
* `film_name`
* ``
